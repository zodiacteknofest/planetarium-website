Horoscope Guide

Discover the mysteries of the zodiac signs

Aries

March 21 - April 19

Placement Info

First sign of the zodiac, ruled by Mars. Fire element, cardinal quality.

Aries Story

Aries represents the ram with the golden fleece from Greek mythology. Bold, pioneering, and full of energy, Aries leads the zodiac with courage and determination.

Taurus

April 20 - May 20

Placement Info

Second sign of the zodiac, ruled by Venus. Earth element, fixed quality.

Taurus Story

The bull that carried Europa across the sea. Taurus represents strength, stability, and the beauty of the material world.

Gemini

May 21 - June 20

Placement Info

Third sign of the zodiac, ruled by Mercury. Air element, mutable quality.

Gemini Story

The twins Castor and Pollux, known for their duality and communication skills. Gemini represents curiosity, adaptability, and the power of information.

Cancer

June 21 - July 22

Placement Info

Fourth sign of the zodiac, ruled by the Moon. Water element, cardinal quality.

Cancer Story

The crab that helped Hydra fight Hercules. Cancer represents home, family, and emotional depth.

Leo

July 23 - August 22

Placement Info

Fifth sign of the zodiac, ruled by the Sun. Fire element, fixed quality.

Leo Story

The Nemean Lion defeated by Hercules. Leo represents courage, leadership, and the radiant power of the sun.

Virgo

August 23 - September 22

Placement Info

Sixth sign of the zodiac, ruled by Mercury. Earth element, mutable quality.

Virgo Story

The maiden associated with harvest and purity. Virgo represents service, attention to detail, and the pursuit of perfection.

Libra

September 23 - October 22

Placement Info

Seventh sign of the zodiac, ruled by Venus. Air element, cardinal quality.

Libra Story

The scales of justice held by Astraea. Libra represents balance, harmony, and the pursuit of fairness.

Scorpio

October 23 - November 21

Placement Info

Eighth sign of the zodiac, ruled by Mars and Pluto. Water element, fixed quality.

Scorpio Story

The scorpion that defeated Orion. Scorpio represents transformation, intensity, and the mysteries of life and death.

Sagittarius

November 22 - December 21

Placement Info

Ninth sign of the zodiac, ruled by Jupiter. Fire element, mutable quality.

Sagittarius Story

The centaur archer, representing the pursuit of knowledge and adventure. Sagittarius embodies optimism, philosophy, and the quest for truth.

Capricorn

December 22 - January 19

Placement Info

Tenth sign of the zodiac, ruled by Saturn. Earth element, cardinal quality.

Capricorn Story

The sea-goat that helped Zeus defeat the Titans. Capricorn represents ambition, discipline, and the mastery of both material and spiritual realms.

Aquarius

January 20 - February 18

Placement Info

Eleventh sign of the zodiac, ruled by Saturn and Uranus. Air element, fixed quality.

Aquarius Story

The water-bearer who brings life to humanity. Aquarius represents innovation, humanitarianism, and the flow of consciousness.

Pisces

February 19 - March 20

Placement Info

Twelfth sign of the zodiac, ruled by Jupiter and Neptune. Water element, mutable quality.

Pisces Story

The two fish swimming in opposite directions, representing the eternal dance between matter and spirit. Pisces embodies compassion, intuition, and the collective unconscious.
